Company Brief

JiangSu Howbetter Food Limited Company was founded in 1998.

By studying food texture and premix technology, we offer product solutions and also sell food additives to dairy, beverage, ice cream and food manufacturers.

Our company has ISO 9001-2015 management system certification, FSSC 22000 system certification, Halal certificated Qualification and equipment allergen cleaning Validation. Our company is the electronic traceable pilot unit of Jiangsu Food and Drug Administration.

Corporate values

Safety, be law-abiding, integrity, respect

Our mission

Try hard to become the No.1 Brand in the industry of China food texture technology.

R&D philosophy

Our purpose is to satisfy customers' needs of getting safe, delicious and healthy products. To achive this purpose, we design our business and also develop our products. We encourage original works.

Enterprise Features
