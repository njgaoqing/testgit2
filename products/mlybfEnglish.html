Soft ice cream premix

Product features:
  • Rich creamy feeling
  • Excellent cold melt
  • Easy-to-use
Method of application:

Dissolving and mixing the product with water or fresh milk（ 1:2.5; Soft ice cream premix:water or milk）, stewing for 30 minutes, putting in ice cream freezing machine, when the freezing is over, you can eat it

Household hands play ice cream premix

Product features:
  • Excellent taste of the ice
  • Easy to operate without freezing machine
Method of application:

Mixing the product with water or fresh milk in a 1: 1.5-2 ratios, whisking 3-5min with a hand-held egg beat, freezing in the freezer, and eat after 8 hours.

Pudding powder

Product features:
  • Rich creamy feeling
  • Exquisite texture
  • Easy-to-operate
Method of application:

Adding the pudding powder with the proportion of 1:3 (powder: water) in water to dissolve, stirring up to 85℃ (almost boiling), Pouring into the mold after cooling, refrigerate until solidified.

Mousse powder

Product features:
  • Rich creamy feeling, just melt in the mouth
  • Excellent expansion rate
  • Light and smooth texture
  • Easy-to-operate
Method of application:

Adding the mousse powder with the proportion of 1: 4 (powder: cold water) in water to dissolve, whisking 3-5min with a hand-held egg beat, filling in the mousse cup in the cold edible , refrigerating until solidified.

Milk tea powder

Product features:
  • Rich creamy feeling
  • Smooth and fragrant
Method of application:

Add the milk powder with the proportion of 1:4-6 (powder: 60-70℃ water) until it is dissolved.
